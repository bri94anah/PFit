Recommitment Tempo Guide

Because you've rested for two weeks, your nervous system is re-adapting. Use **Slow Controlled Tension** rather than heavy loads to avoid excessive next-day soreness.

The 3 - 1 - 1 tempo system:

  • 3s Descent: Lower the weight stack down counting 1, 2, 3 slowly.
  • 1s Pause: Active contraction hold at the bottom of the movement.
  • 1s Lift: Exhale and push the weight up smoothly.
