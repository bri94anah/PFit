PF Machine Reference

Quick tips to master your weight progression safely on the gym floor.

The "Half-Weight" Dial Hook

Most machine stacks at Planet Fitness have standard jumps of 10 lbs or 15 lbs. On the top of the stack, look for a small slider, selector dial, or a tiny add-on plate. These are **micro-adjusters of 2.5 lbs or 5 lbs**. Always use these to make tiny, joint-friendly increments!

The Double Progression Rule

Master your *reps* before touching the weight. If an exercise is scheduled for **8–12 reps**, do not increase the weight until you can easily complete **all 12 reps on all 3 sets** with perfect form. Once you achieve this, add the smallest weight increment, and drop back down to 8 reps.

Recommended Safe Increases

Leg Machines Add 5 to 10 lbs
Chest & Back Add 5 lbs
Shoulders & Arms Add 2.5 lbs (Cables)
Dumbbell Weights Aim for 15 reps first
